THE GREAT SAHYADRI ESCAPE

Expedition Blueprint

A raw 4-day motorcycle journey from Pragathi Nagar, Hyderabad to the hidden gems of Kumbhe Plateau — featuring a 178ft waterfall, an ancient hand-carved tunnel, and the misty Tamhini Ghat. This is not just a ride; it's a full-spectrum adventure through the Sahyadri's best-kept secrets.

Daily Itinerary

📅 Day 1 – The Highway Push (570 km)
05:00 AM flag-off from Pragathi Nagar (Kukatpally). NH65 express run via Zaheerabad, Solapur. Breakfast at Hotel Sai Palace, Solapur. Fuel stops every 150km. Reach Pune by 5:00 PM. Overnight at Rider's Hostel, Koregaon Park. Evening: bike check, chain lube, group dinner.
📅 Day 2 – Into the Wild Ghats (130 km)
08:00 AM departure from Pune → Mulshi Dam (photo stop) → Tamhini Ghat (40km of pure twisties, 12 hairpin bends, roadside waterfalls). Reach Mangaon by 2 PM. Devkund trek (3km jungle trail to turquoise lagoon). Overnight riverside camping with bonfire.
📅 Day 3 – Kumbhe Core Expedition (60 km offroad)
07:00 AM: Ride to Kumbhe base. 10km dirt trail to Ancient Rock Tunnel – a 1km hand-carved cave. After tunnel, ascend to Kumbhe Plateau to witness 178ft waterfall plunge. Packed lunch at falls. Nanemachi Amphitheater (circular rock wall). Return to Mangaon by 6 PM.
📅 Day 4 – The Return Ride (700 km)
05:00 AM sharp departure. Breakfast at Lonavala. Fuel stops at Pune, Solapur. Reach Pragathi Nagar by 9:00 PM. Victory dinner (optional).

Terrain Intelligence

Tamhini Ghat: Wet, low visibility in rains – use fog lights, reduce speed.

Kumbhe Offroad: Loose gravel, rocky sections, stream crossing. Stand on pegs.

Last Fuel: Mangaon (fill reserve can if possible). Emergency Fuel: Local shops in Kumbhe village may have petrol at premium.

Mobile Network: Patchy beyond Kumbhe base – offline maps essential.
